About Us

Welcome to Sizzling Delights

Welcome to Sizzling Delights, where we've been serving up mouthwatering India, italian and american dishes in Mysore since 2004. Our passion for fresh, flavorful food is at the heart of everything we do.

Our cozy, intimate atmosphere provides the perfect setting to enjoy a romantic dinner, catch up with friends over cocktails, or simply indulge in a delicious meal after a long day. We look forward to welcoming you through our doors soon!
